# Banner Images Guidelines

## Image Specifications

### Background Images
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (16:9 aspect ratio) minimum
- **Format**: JPG or WebP preferred
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Quality**: High resolution but web-optimized

### Naming Convention
- `slide-1-bg.jpg` - First slide background
- `slide-2-bg.jpg` - Second slide background  
- `slide-3-bg.jpg` - Third slide background

## Image Requirements

1. **Visual Style**: Professional, modern, urban-focused
2. **Color Scheme**: Should work well with overlay (teal/blue gradient)
3. **Content**: Urban development, learning, collaboration themes
4. **Composition**: Leave space for text content on the left side

## Recommended Image Sources

- Unsplash (unsplash.com) - Free high-quality stock photos
- Pexels (pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- Your own photography related to urban development

## Example Search Terms

- "urban planning meeting"
- "city development"
- "modern office collaboration"
- "professional team working"
- "urban skyline"
- "architectural planning"

## Adding Images

1. Place your images in this folder (`public/images/banner/`)
2. Name them according to the convention above
3. The banner component will automatically use them
4. Images will have a blue/teal overlay applied automatically
